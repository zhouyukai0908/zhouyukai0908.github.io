2034

2034 is a 360/VR micro-movie that envisions a world transformed by advanced AI technologies. Through immersive and interactive experiences, this project explores various futuristic scenarios, from smart homes to virtual workplaces. It demonstrates how AI and VR technologies could revolutionize daily life, providing a thought-provoking glimpse into a future where technology seamlessly integrates into our lives, enhancing convenience and connectivity.
